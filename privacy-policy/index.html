DoReMingo

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

DoReMingo is an ear-training game published by OK Do! Ltd, available as an app and as a web game at doremingo.com. We have built it to collect no personal data at all. The only thing we measure is an anonymous count of visits, described below. This policy explains exactly what that means.

Data we collect

No personal data. DoReMingo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information about you. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no contact forms.

Anonymous visit counting

To know roughly how many people play, the game and this website use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first measurement service. It tells us aggregate numbers — how many times a page was loaded, from which country, and with which browser type. It works without cookies and without identifying you:

  • no cookies and nothing stored on your device;
  • no fingerprinting and no persistent identifiers;
  • no tracking of individuals across pages, sites, or visits;
  • your IP address is processed transiently to serve the request and derive a country, and is not stored or shown to us.

We cannot see who you are, only that someone played. This applies to the web game and to the app when it is online.

Network and tracking

Beyond the anonymous visit counting above, the game sends no data off your device and contains:

  • no other analytics or usage tracking;
  • no advertising;
  • no cookies or third-party SDKs;
  • no crash or telemetry reporting.

In the app, all sounds and content are bundled inside it and play entirely offline. On the web, your browser fetches the game's files from our static host and nothing more.

Information stored on your device

The game saves your progress (such as which levels you have completed, or your daily-puzzle streak) locally on your device only — in the app's own storage, or in your browser's local storage on the web. This information never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is removed if you delete the app or clear your browser data.

Children's privacy

DoReMingo is suitable for all ages. Because the game collects no personal data whatsoever, it collects nothing from children either.

Changes to this policy

If the game's data practices ever change, we will update this page and revise the date above before the change ships.
