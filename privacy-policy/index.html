DoReMingo

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

DoReMingo is an ear-training game published by OK Do! Ltd, available as an app and as a web game at doremingo.com. We have built it to collect no personal data at all. This policy explains exactly what that means.

Data we collect

None. DoReMingo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information about you. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no contact forms.

Network and tracking

The game does not send any data off your device. It makes no network requests to us or to anyone else, and contains:

  • no analytics or usage tracking;
  • no advertising;
  • no cookies or third-party SDKs;
  • no crash or telemetry reporting.

In the app, all sounds and content are bundled inside it and play entirely offline. On the web, your browser fetches the game's files from our static host and nothing more.

Information stored on your device

The game saves your progress (such as which levels you have completed, or your daily-puzzle streak) locally on your device only — in the app's own storage, or in your browser's local storage on the web. This information never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is removed if you delete the app or clear your browser data.

Children's privacy

DoReMingo is suitable for all ages. Because the game collects no data whatsoever, it collects nothing from children either.

Changes to this policy

If the game's data practices ever change, we will update this page and revise the date above before the change ships.
