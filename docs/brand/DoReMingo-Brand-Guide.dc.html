07 — Mingo the flamingo

Four moods, all built from soft shapes.

Mingo is drawn entirely from circles, ellipses and rounded strokes — no fine illustration, so it scales and animates cleanly. Even Sad stays gentle: a small frown, a single tear, never distress.

10 — Level screen, redesigned

In-game: back where it belongs, Mingo where he matters.

Three fixes from the live build: the ambiguous 🗺️ map glyph becomes a plain back chevron top-left; the question + answers vertically centre into the thumb zone; and Mingo stops floating — he stands centre-bottom and talks up through a speech bubble, so every reaction comes from one friendly place.

← Back, top-left
A standard chevron = “leave this screen.” The progress bar fills the rest of the row; no icon doing double duty.
🦩 Mingo anchors the base
Centre-bottom, filling the dead space. Feedback rises above him in a bubble; he shifts idle → party → droop with the answer.
↕ Fits small screens
Everything between nav and Mingo is vertically centred and scales — on an SE-class phone it all fits, no scroll.
