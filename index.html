DoReMingo is a free daily music puzzle. Listen to a hidden tune, play it back in Do‑Re‑Mi, and share your grid — spoiler-free. Same melody for everyone, new one at midnight. No sign-up, no music training needed.

How it works

  1. Do
    Listen. A short melody plays, hidden behind the board. Hear it again as often as you like.
  2. Re
    Echo it. Tap the notes you heard in Do‑Re‑Mi. Green means right note, right spot; yellow means it's in the tune, wrong spot.
  3. Mi
    Share your grid. The coloured squares show how you did without giving the tune away. Can your friends beat it?

Run out of guesses? The melody is revealed and played for you — every puzzle teaches your ear something.

The Daily is just the warm-up

The DoReMingo app has the whole journey: twelve Learn levels that take you from your very first Do to hearing melodies in any key, and a quick Warmup to tune your ear before the Daily. It's real ear training (Kodály movable-do) dressed up as a game — no fail states, works offline, collects no personal data.

  • 🎧 Learn — 12 levels, from the tonic triad to chromatic notes and new keys
  • 🔥 Daily — keep your streak and stats on your phone
  • 🌤️ Warmup — a two-minute pentatonic ear-tune
